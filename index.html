Short Bio

​I am currently a second-year M.Eng. student in the College of ​Computer Science and Technology, Nanjing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics, supervised by Prof. Qi Zhu. Previously, I received the B. Eng. degree from Jimei University, in 2022.

My research interest lies in affective computing, multi-modal learning, and biomedical signal analysis.
